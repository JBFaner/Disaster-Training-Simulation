**Primary Focus:** I-adjust ang complexity based sa difficulty level

**Ano ang ginagawa:**
- **Basic:** Simple scenarios, clear instructions
- **Intermediate:** Moderate complexity, some challenges
- **Advanced:** Complex scenarios, multiple challenges, cascading failures

**Example:**
- Basic: Single disaster, clear evacuation route
- Advanced: Multi-hazard (earthquake + fire + power outage), blocked routes, communication failure

---

**Primary Focus:** Real-world challenges na makikita ng LGU personnel

**Ano ang ginagawa:**
- Resource limitations (limited equipment, personnel)
- Communication issues (network down, radio problems)
- Evacuation challenges (crowded areas, blocked exits)
- Decision-making under pressure

**Why Important:**
- Hindi theoretical - practical challenges
- Based sa actual LGU operations
- Realistic resource constraints

---

### **5. STRUCTURED OUTPUT FOR TRAINING** ‚≠ê
**Primary Focus:** Generate complete scenario structure para sa training

**Ano ang ginagawa:**
- **Title:** Clear, descriptive scenario name
- **Description:** Detailed 2-3 paragraph scenario
- **Initial Conditions:** Starting situation
- **Challenges:** List of challenges personnel must address
- **Expected Actions:** What personnel should do
- **Safety Notes:** Important warnings
- **Learning Objectives:** What trainees should learn

**Why Important:**
- Complete scenario structure
- Ready to use for training
- Includes evaluation criteria (expected actions)
